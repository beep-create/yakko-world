Bolivia, officially the Plurinational State of Bolivia,is a landlocked country located in western-central South America. <br>
 The constitutional capital is Sucre, while the seat of government and executive capital is La Paz. <br>
 The largest city and principal industrial center is Santa Cruz de la Sierra, located on the Llanos Orientales (tropical lowlands), <br>
 a mostly flat region in the east of the country. <br>
The sovereign state of Bolivia is a constitutionally unitary state, divided into nine departments. <br>
Its geography varies from the peaks of the Andes in the West, to the Eastern Lowlands, situated within the Amazon basin. <br>
It is bordered to the north and east by Brazil, to the southeast by Paraguay, to the south by Argentina, <br>
to the southwest by Chile, and to the northwest by Peru. <br>
One-third of the country is within the Andean mountain range. <br>
With 1,098,581 km2 (424,164 sq mi) of area, Bolivia is the fifth largest country in South America, <br>
and alongside Paraguay, one of the only two landlocked countries in the Americas. <br>

China, officially the People's Republic of China, is a country in East Asia. <br>
It is the world's most populous country, with a population of more than 1.4 billion. <br>
China follows a single standard time offset of UTC+08:00 even though spanning five geographical time zones and borders 14 countries, <br>
the second most of any country in the world, after Russia. <br>
Covering an area of approximately 9.6 million square kilometers (3.7 million mi2), it is the world's third or fourth largest country.<br>
 The nation has the world's largest standing army — the People's Liberation Army — the second-largest defense budget, <br>
 and is a recognized nuclear weapons state. <br>
 China has been characterized as a potential superpower due to its large economy and powerful military.<br>

Djibouti,[a] officially the Republic of Djibouti, is a country located in the Horn of Africa. <br>
It is bordered by Somaliland in the south, Ethiopia in the southwest, Eritrea in the north,<br>
 and the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden in the east, Across the Gulf of Aden is Yemen. <br>
 The country has a total area of 23,200 km2 (8,958 sq mi).<br>
  French and Arabic are the country's two official languages, Afar and Somali are national languages. <br>
  About 94% of residents adhere to Islam, which is the official religion and has been predominant in the region for more than a thousand years. <br>

Ghana, officially the Republic of Ghana, is a country in West Africa. <br>
It spans the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean to the south, sharing borders with the Ivory Coast in the west,  <br>
Burkina Faso in the north, and Togo in the east. <br>
 Ghana covers an area of 238,535 km2 (92,099 sq mi), spanning a diverse geography and ecology that ranges from coastal savannahs to tropical rain forests. <br> 
 With over 31 million people, Ghana is the second-most populous country in West Africa, after Nigeria.  <br>
 The capital and largest city is Accra; other major cities include Kumasi, Tamale, and Sekondi-Takoradi. <br>

El Salvador, officially the Republic of El Salvador, is a country in Central America.  <br>
It is bordered on the northeast by Honduras, on the northwest by Guatemala, and on the south by the Pacific Ocean.  <br>
El Salvador's capital and largest city is San Salvador.  <br>
As of 2021, the country had a population of approximately 6,825,935. <br>
El Salvador ranks 14th among Latin American countries in terms of the Human Development Index <br>
and fourth in Central America partly because of ongoing rapid industrialization. <br>
However, the country continues to struggle with high rates of poverty, inequality, and gang-related violent crime. <br>

Azerbaijan, officially the Republic of Azerbaijan, is a country located at the boundary of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. <br>
 It is a part of the Caucasus region, and is bounded by the Caspian Sea to the east, Russia to the north, <br>
 Georgia to the northwest, Armenia and Turkey to the west, and Iran to the south. <br>
 Baku is the capital and largest city. <br>
 Azerbaijan has a total area of 86,600 km2 (33,400 sq mi). <br>
 The total population of Azerbaijan, according to the 2021 estimate is 10,130,100. <br>

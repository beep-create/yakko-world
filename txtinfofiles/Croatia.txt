Croatia, officially the Republic of Croatia, is a country at the crossroads of Central and Southeast Europe on the Adriatic Sea.<br>
 It borders Slovenia to the northwest, Hungary to the northeast, Serbia to the east, <br>
 Bosnia and Herzegovina and Montenegro to the southeast, and shares a maritime border with Italy to the west and southwest. <br>
 Its capital and largest city, Zagreb, forms one of the country's primary subdivisions, with twenty counties. <br>
 Croatia has 56,594 square kilometres (21,851 square miles) and a population of 4.07 million.<br>
 The currency is the Croatian Kuna. <br>

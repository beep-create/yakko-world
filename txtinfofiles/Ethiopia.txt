Ethiopia, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia, is a landlocked country in the Horn of Africa. <br>
It shares borders with Eritrea to the north, Djibouti and Somaliland to the northeast, Somalia to the east, <br>
Kenya to the south, South Sudan to the west and Sudan to the northwest. <br>
Ethiopia has a total area of 1,100,000 square kilometres (420,000 sq mi) and over 117 million inhabitants<br>
 and is the 12th-most populous country in the world and the 2nd-most populous in Africa. <br>
 The national capital and largest city, Addis Ababa, lies several kilometres west of the East African Rift <br>
 that splits the country into the African and Somali tectonic plates.<br>

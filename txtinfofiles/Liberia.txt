Liberia, officially the Republic of Liberia, is a country on the West African coast. <br>
It is bordered by Sierra Leone to its northwest, Guinea to its north, Ivory Coast to its east, <br>
and the Atlantic Ocean to its south and southwest. <br>
It has a population of around 5 million and covers an area of 111,369 square kilometers (43,000 sq mi). <br>
English is the official language, but over 20 indigenous languages are spoken, reflecting the country's ethnic and cultural diversity. <br>
The country's capital and largest city is Monrovia. <br>

Moldova, officially the Republic of Moldova, is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe.<br>
 It is bordered by Romania to the west and Ukraine to the north, east, and south.<br>
  Additionally, the unrecognised state of Transnistria, internationally recognised as part of Moldova, <br>
  lies across the Dniester on the country's eastern Ukrainian border. <br>
  The capital and largest city is Chișinău.<br>
  It is the second poorest country in Europe by GDP per capita.<br>
   Although Moldova has a relatively high Human Development Index, it is the lowest in the continent, ranking 90th in the world.<br>

Austria, officially the Republic of Austria, is a landlocked East Alpine country in the southern part of Central Europe. <br>
 It is composed of nine federated states, one of which is Vienna, Austria's capital and largest city. <br>
 It is bordered by Germany to the northwest, the Czech Republic to the north, Slovakia to the northeast, Hungary to the east, <br>
 Slovenia and Italy to the south, and Switzerland and Liechtenstein to the west. <br>
 Austria occupies an area of 83,879 km2 (32,386 sq mi) and has a population of nearly 9 million people. <br>
 While German is the country's official language, many Austrians communicate informally in a variety of Bavarian dialects. <br>
 The currency of Austria is the Euro. <br>

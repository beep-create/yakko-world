Bhutan, officially known as the Kingdom of Bhutan, is a landlocked country in the Eastern Himalayas. <br>
It is bordered by China to the north and India to the south. <br>
Nepal and Bangladesh are located in proximity to Bhutan but do not share a land border. <br>
The country has a population of over 754,000 and a territory of 38,394 square kilometers (14,824 sq mi) which ranks 133rd in terms of land area, and 160th in population. <br>
Bhutan is a constitutional monarchy with Vajrayana Buddhism as the state religion. <br>
The currency of Bhutan is the Ngultrum. <br>

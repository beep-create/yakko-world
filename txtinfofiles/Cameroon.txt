Cameroon, officially the Republic of Cameroon, is a country in west-central Africa. <br>
It is bordered by Nigeria to the west and north; Chad to the northeast; the Central African Republic to the east; <br>
and Equatorial Guinea, Gabon and the Republic of the Congo to the south. <br>
Its coastline lies on the Bight of Biafra, part of the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean. <br>
The country is sometimes identified as West African and other times as Central African, due to its strategic position at the crossroads between West and Central Africa. <br>
Its nearly 25 million people speak 250 native languages. <br>
Cameroons total area is 475,442 km (183,569 sq mi). <br>
The official currency of Cameroon is the Central African CFA Franc.

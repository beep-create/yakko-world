Barbados is an island country in the Lesser Antilles of the West Indies, in the Caribbean region of North America, and the most easterly of the Caribbean Islands.  <br>
It is 34 kilometres (21 miles) in length and up to 23 km (14 mi) in width, covering an area of 432 km2 (167 sq mi). <br>
It is in the western part of the North Atlantic, 100 km (62 mi) east of the Windward Islands and the Caribbean Sea.  <br>

 It is about 168 km (104 mi) east of both the countries of Saint Lucia and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines  <br>
 and 180 km (110 mi) south-east of Martinique and 400 km (250 mi) north-east of Trinidad and Tobago.  <br>
 Barbados is outside the principal Atlantic hurricane belt. Its capital and largest city is Bridgetown.  <br>
 The currency of Barbados is the Barbadian dollar. <br>

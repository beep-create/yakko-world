Jamaica is an island country situated in the Caribbean Sea. <br>
Spanning 10,990 square kilometres (4,240 sq mi) in area, it is the third-largest island of the Greater Antilles and the Caribbean (after Cuba and Hispaniola).<br>
Jamaica lies about 145 kilometres (90 mi) south of Cuba, and 191 kilometres (119 mi) west of Hispaniola;<br>
 the British Overseas Territory of the Cayman Islands lies some 215 kilometres (134 mi) to the north-west.<br>
 With 2.9 million people, Jamaica is the third-most populous Anglophone country in the Americas, and the fourth-most populous country in the Caribbean. <br>
 Kingston is the country's capital and largest city.<br>

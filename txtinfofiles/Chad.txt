Chad, officially known as the Republic of Chad, is a landlocked country in north-central Africa. <br>
It is bordered by Libya to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, <br>
Cameroon to the south-west, Nigeria to the southwest (at Lake Chad), and Niger to the west. <br>
It has a population of 16 million, of which 1.6 million live in the capital and largest city N'Djamena. <br>
Chad's official languages are Arabic and French. <br>
It is home to over 200 different ethnic and linguistic groups. <br>
Islam (51.8%) and Christianity (44.1%) are the main religions practiced in Chad. <br>

Greece, officially the Hellenic Republic, is a country located in Southeast Europe. <br>
Its population is approximately 10.7 million as of 2018; Athens is its largest and capital city, followed by Thessaloniki.<br>
 Situated on the southern tip of the Balkans, Greece is located at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa. <br>
 It shares land borders with Albania to the northwest, North Macedonia and Bulgaria to the north, and Turkey to the northeast. <br>
 The Aegean Sea lies to the east of the mainland, the Ionian Sea to the west, the Cretan Sea and the Mediterranean Sea to the south.<br> 
 Greece has the longest coastline on the Mediterranean Basin and the 11th longest coastline in the world <br>
 at 13,676 km (8,498 mi) in length, featuring many islands, of which 227 are inhabited. <br>
 Eighty percent of Greece is mountainous, with Mount Olympus being the highest peak at 2,918 metres (9,573 ft). <br>

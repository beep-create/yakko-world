Mauritius, officially the Republic of Mauritius, is an island nation in the Indian Ocean about 2,000 kilometres (1,200 mi) <br>
off the southeast coast of the African continent, east of Madagascar.  <br>
It includes the main island (also called Mauritius), as well as Rodrigues, Agaléga and St. Brandon. <br>
 The islands of Mauritius and Rodrigues, along with nearby Réunion (a French overseas department), are part of the Mascarene Islands.  <br>
 The capital and largest city, Port Louis, is located in Mauritius, where most of the population is concentrated.  <br>
 The country spans 2,040 square kilometres (790 sq mi) and has an exclusive economic zone covering 2.3 million square kilometres. <br>

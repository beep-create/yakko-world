Gabon, is a country on the west coast of Central Africa. <br>
Located on the equator, Gabon is bordered by Equatorial Guinea to the northwest, Cameroon to the north, <br>
the Republic of the Congo on the east and south, and the Gulf of Guinea to the west. <br>
It has an area of nearly 270,000 square kilometres (100,000 sq mi) and its population is estimated at 2.1 million people. <br>
There are three distinct regions: the coastal plains, the mountains (the Cristal Mountains and the Chaillu Massif in the centre), and the savanna in the east.<br> 
Gabon's capital and largest city is Libreville. <br>
The official language is French.<br>

East Timor, or Timor-Leste, officially the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste, is an island country in Southeast Asia.<br>
 It comprises the eastern half of the island of Timor, the nearby islands of Atauro and Jaco,<br>
  and Oecusse, an exclave on the northwestern side of the island surrounded by Indonesian West Timor.<br> 
  Australia is the country's southern neighbour, separated by the Timor Sea. <br>
  The country's size is about 15,007 square kilometres (5,794 sq mi).<br>
  The currency of East Timor is the US Dollar. <br>

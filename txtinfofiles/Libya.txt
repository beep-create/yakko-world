Libya, officially the State of Libya, is a country in the Maghreb region in North Africa bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north,  <br>
Egypt to the east, Sudan to the southeast, Chad to the south, Niger to the southwest, Algeria to the west and Tunisia to the northwest.  <br>
The sovereign state is made of three historical regions: Tripolitania, Fezzan and Cyrenaica.  <br>
With an area of almost 700,000 square miles (1.8 million km2), Libya is the fourth largest country in Africa and is the 16th largest country in the world. <br>
 Libya has the 10th-largest proven oil reserves of any country in the world. <br>
  The largest city and capital, Tripoli, is located in western Libya and contains over three million of Libya's seven million people. <br>

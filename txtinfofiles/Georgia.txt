Georgia is a country located at the intersection of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. <br>
It is a part of the Caucasus region, bounded to the west by the Black Sea, to the north and east by Russia,<br>
 to the south by Turkey and Armenia, and to the southeast by Azerbaijan. <br>
 It covers 69,700 square kilometres (26,911 sq mi), and has a population of around 4 million.<br>
  Georgia is a representative democracy governed as a unitary parliamentary republic.<br>
   Tbilisi is the capital and largest city, home to roughly a quarter of the population. <br>

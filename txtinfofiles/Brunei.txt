Brunei, officially the Nation of Brunei, the Abode of Peace, is a country located on the north coast of the island of Borneo in Southeast Asia. <br>
Apart from its coastline with the South China Sea, the country is completely surrounded by the Malaysian state of Sarawak. <br>
It is separated into two parts by the Sarawak district of Limbang. <br>
 Brunei is the only sovereign state completely on the island of Borneo.    <br>
 The remainder of the island's territory is divided between the nations of Malaysia and Indonesia. <br>
 Brunei's population was 460,345 in 2020, of which about 100,000 live in the capital and largest city Bandar Seri Begawan. <br>
 The government is an absolute monarchy under the sovereign rule of Sultan and Yang di-Pertuan, 
 which implements a combination of English common law and sharia law, as well as direct general Islamic practices.<br>

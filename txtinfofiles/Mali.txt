Mali  officially the Republic of Mali, is a landlocked country in West Africa. <br>
Mali is the eighth-largest country in Africa, with an area of just over 1,240,000 square kilometres (480,000 sq mi).<br>
 The population of Mali is 19.1 million, 67% of its population was estimated to be under the age of 25 in 2017.<br>
Its capital and largest city is Bamako. <br>
The sovereign state of Mali consists of eight regions and its borders on the north reach deep into the middle of the Sahara Desert. <br>
The country's southern part is in the Sudanian savanna, where the majority of inhabitants live, and both the Niger and Senegal rivers pass through. <br>
The country's economy centres on agriculture and mining. <br>
One of Mali's most prominent natural resources is gold, and the country is the third largest producer of gold on the African continent.<br>

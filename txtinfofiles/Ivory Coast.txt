Ivory Coast, officially the Republic of Côte d'Ivoire, is a country located on the south coast of West Africa. <br>
Ivory Coast's political capital is Yamoussoukro in the centre of the country, while its economic capital and largest city is the port city of Abidjan. <br>
It borders Guinea to the northwest, Liberia to the west, Mali to the northwest, Burkina Faso to the northeast, <br>
Ghana to the east, and the Gulf of Guinea (Atlantic Ocean) to the south. <br>
The official language of the republic is French, with local indigenous languages also being widely used that include Baoulé, Dioula, Dan, Anyin, and Cebaara Senufo. <br>
n total, there are around 78 different languages spoken in Ivory Coast. <br>
The country has large populations of the adherents of Christianity, Islam and various indigenous religions. <br>

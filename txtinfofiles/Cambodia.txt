Cambodia, officially the Kingdom of Cambodia, is a country located in the southern portion of the Indochinese Peninsula in Southeast Asia. <br>
It is 181,035 square kilometres (69,898 square miles) in area, bordered by Thailand to the northwest, Laos to the north, <br>
Vietnam to the east and the Gulf of Thailand to the southwest. <br>
Phnom Penh is the nation's capital and largest city. <br>
The sovereign state of Cambodia has a population of over 15 million. <br>
Buddhism is enshrined in the constitution as the official state religion, and is practised by more than 97% of the population. <br>
Cambodias official currency is the Riel. <br>

Andorra, officially the Principality of Andorra, is a sovereign landlocked microstate on the Iberian Peninsula, <br>
in the eastern Pyrenees, bordered by France to the north and Spain to the south. <br>
 The present principality was formed by a charter in 1278. <br>
 It is headed by two co-princes: the Bishop of Urgell in Catalonia, Spain and the President of France. <br>
 Its capital and largest city is Andorra la Vella. <br>
 Andorra is not a member state of the European Union, but the euro is its official currency. <br>
 It has been a member of the United Nations since 1993. <br>
 In 2013,  Andorra had the highest life expectancy in the world at 81 years, according to the Global Burden of Disease Study. <br>

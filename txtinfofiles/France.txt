France, is a non-contiguous transcontinental country consisting of metropolitan France in Western Europe and several overseas regions and territories.<br>
 The metropolitan area of France extends from the Rhine to the Atlantic Ocean and from the Mediterranean Sea to the English Channel and the North Sea. <br>
 The overseas territories include French Guiana in South America and several islands in the Atlantic, Pacific and Indian Oceans.<br>
 France borders Belgium, Luxembourg and Germany to the northeast, Switzerland, Monaco and Italy to the east, <br>
 Andorra and Spain to the south, as well as the Netherlands, Suriname and Brazil in the Americas. <br>
 The country's eighteen integral regions (five of which are situated overseas) span a combined area of 643,801 km2 (248,573 sq mi) <br>
 and a total population of 67.413 million (as of May 2021). <br>
 France is a unitary semi-presidential republic with its capital in Paris, the country's largest city and main cultural and commercial centre. <br>
 Other major urban areas include Lyon, Marseille, Toulouse, Bordeaux, Lille and Nice. <br>
 France, including its overseas territories, has the most time zones of any country, with a total of twelve. <br>

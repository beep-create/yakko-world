Botswana, officially the Republic of Botswana, is a landlocked country in Southern Africa. <br>
Botswana is topographically flat, with up to 70 percent of its territory being the Kalahari Desert. <br>
 It is bordered by South Africa to the south and southeast, Namibia to the west and north, and Zimbabwe to the northeast. <br>
 It it connected to Zambia across the short Zambezi River border by the Kazungula Bridge. <br>
A mid-sized country of slightly over 2.3 million people, it is one of the most sparsely populated countries in the world. <br>
About 11.6 percent of the population lives in the capital and largest city, Gaborone. <br>

The Czech Republic, also known by its short-form name, Czechia, is a landlocked country in Central Europe.<br>
 It is bordered by Austria to the south, Germany to the west, Poland to the northeast, and Slovakia to the east.<br>
  The Czech Republic has a hilly landscape that covers an area of 78,871 square kilometers (30,452 sq mi) <br>
  with a mostly temperate continental and oceanic climate.<br>
  The Czech Republic is a member of NATO, the EU, OECD, OSCE, and the CoE. <br>
  The currency of the Czech Republic is the Czech Koruna. <br>

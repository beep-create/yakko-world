Malaysia  is a country in Southeast Asia. <br>
The federal constitutional monarchy consists of thirteen states and three federal territories, <br>
separated by the South China Sea into two regions, Peninsular Malaysia and Borneo's East Malaysia. <br>
Peninsular Malaysia shares a land and maritime border with Thailand and maritime borders with Singapore, Vietnam, and Indonesia.<br>
East Malaysia shares land and maritime borders with Brunei and Indonesia and a maritime border with the Philippines and Vietnam.<br>
 Kuala Lumpur is the national capital and largest city while Putrajaya is the seat of the federal government. <br>
 With a population of over 32 million, Malaysia is the world's 43rd-most populous country. <br>
 The southernmost point of continental Eurasia is in Tanjung Piai. <br>
 In the tropics, Malaysia is one of 17 megadiverse countries, home to a number of endemic species.<br>

Brazil, officially the Federative Republic of Brazil, is the largest country in both South America and Latin America. <br>
It covers an area of 8,515,767 square kilometres (3,287,956 sq mi), with a population of over 211 million. <br>
Brazil is the world's fifth-largest and sixth-most populous country, composed of 26 states and the Federal District. <br>
It is the largest country to have Portuguese as an official language and the only one in the Americas. <br>
 Brazil is one of the world's most multicultural and ethnically diverse nations, due to over a century of mass immigration from around the world. <br>
  It is also the most populous Roman Catholic-majority country, and its capital is Brasília, while the largest city is São Paulo. <br>
  The currency is the Brazillian Real. <br>

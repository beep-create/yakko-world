The Central African Republic, is a landlocked country in Central Africa. <br>
It is bordered by Chad to the north, Sudan to the northeast, South Sudan to the southeast, the DR Congo to the south, <br>
the Republic of the Congo to the southwest, and Cameroon to the west.<br>
The CAR covers a land area of about 620,000 square kilometres (240,000 sq mi). <br>
As of 2018, it had an estimated population of around 4.7 million. <br>
 As of 2021, the CAR is the scene of a civil war, ongoing since 2012. <br>
 Most of the CAR consists of Sudano-Guinean savannas, but the country also includes a Sahelo-Sudanian zone in the north and an equatorial forest zone in the south. <br>

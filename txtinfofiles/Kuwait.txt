Kuwait, officially the State of Kuwait, is a country in Western Asia. <br>
It is situated in the northern edge of Eastern Arabia at the tip of the Persian Gulf, bordering Iraq to the north and Saudi Arabia to the south. <br>
Kuwait has a coastal length of approximately 499 km (310 mi).<br>
 As of 2021, Kuwait has a population of 4.5 million people where 1.3 million are Kuwaitis and 3.2 million are foreign nationals.<br>
  Most of the country's population reside in the urban agglomeration of the capital city Kuwait City.<br>
  The Kuwaiti dinar is the highest valued currency in the world.<br>
   Kuwait is the fifth richest country in the world by gross national income per capita.<br>

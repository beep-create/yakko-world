The Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC), formerly Zaire, is a country in Central Africa.  <br>
It is, by area, the largest country in sub-Saharan Africa, the second-largest in all of Africa (after Algeria), and the 11th-largest in the world.  <br>
 With a population of around 105 million, the Democratic Republic of the Congo is the most populous officially Francophone country in the world,  <br>
 as well as the third-most populous country in Africa (after Nigeria and Ethiopia) and the 14th-most populous country in the world.  <br>
 It is a member of the United Nations, Non-Aligned Movement, African Union, and COMESA.  <br>
 Since 2015, the Eastern DR Congo has been the site of an ongoing military conflict in Kivu.  <br>
 The capital and largest city is Kinshasa. <br>

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. <br>
It is the second-most populous country, the seventh-largest country by land area, and the most populous democracy in the world.<br>
 Bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, the Arabian Sea on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast, <br>
 it shares land borders with Pakistan to the west; China, Nepal, and Bhutan to the north; and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. <br>
 In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives;  <br>
 its Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand, Myanmar and Indonesia. <br>
 India has been a federal republic since 1950, governed in a democratic parliamentary system.  <br>
 It is a pluralistic, multilingual and multi-ethnic society.  <br>
 India's population grew from 361 million in 1951 to 1.211 billion in 2011. <br>
  During the same time, its nominal per capita income increased from US$64 annually to US$1,498, and its literacy rate from 16.6% to 74%.  <br>

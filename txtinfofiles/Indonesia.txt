Indonesia, officially the Republic of Indonesia is a country in Southeast Asia and Oceania between the Indian and Pacific oceans.<br>
 It consists of over seventeen thousand islands, including Sumatra, Java, Sulawesi, and parts of Borneo and New Guinea (Papua).<br>
 Indonesia is the world's largest island country and the 14th-largest country by land area, at 1,904,569 square kilometres (735,358 square miles).<br>
  With more than 270 million people, Indonesia is the world's fourth-most populous country and the most populous Muslim-majority country. <br>
  Java, the world's most populous island, is home to more than half of the country's population.<br>
  Indonesia consists of hundreds of distinct native ethnic and linguistic groups, with Javanese being the largest.<br>

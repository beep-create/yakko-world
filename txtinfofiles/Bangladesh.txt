Bangladesh, officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh, is a country in South Asia. <br>
It is the eighth-most populous country in the world, with a population exceeding 163 million people, in an area of 148,560 square kilometres, <br>
making it one of the most densely populated countries in the world. <br>
Bangladesh shares land borders with India to the west, north, and east, Myanmar to the southeast, and the Bay of Bengal to the south. <br>
It is narrowly separated from Nepal and Bhutan by the Siliguri Corridor, and from China by the Indian state of Sikkim in the north, <br>
respectively. Dhaka, the capital and largest city, is the nation's economic, political, and cultural hub. <br>
Chittagong, the largest seaport is the second-largest city. <br>
The currency of Bangladesh is the Bengali Taka. <br>

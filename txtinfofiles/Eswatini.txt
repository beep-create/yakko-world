Eswatini, officially the Kingdom of Eswatini, formerly known as Swaziland, is a landlocked country in Southern Africa.<br>
 It is bordered by Mozambique to its northeast and South Africa to its north, west, and south. <br>
 At no more than 200 kilometres (120 mi) north to south and 130 kilometres (81 mi) east to west, Eswatini is one of the smallest countries in Africa; <br>
 despite this, its climate and topography are diverse, ranging from a cool and mountainous highveld to a hot and dry lowveld.<br>
 The capitals of Eswatini are Mbabane (executive), and Lobamba (legislative). <br>

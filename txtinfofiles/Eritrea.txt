Eritrea, officially the State of Eritrea, is a country in Eastern Africa, with its capital (and largest city) at Asmara. <br>
It is bordered by Ethiopia in the south, Sudan in the west, and Djibouti in the southeast. <br>
The northeastern and eastern parts of Eritrea have an extensive coastline along the Red Sea. <br>
The nation has a total area of approximately 117,600 km2 (45,406 sq mi), and includes the Dahlak Archipelago and several of the Hanish Islands.<br>
According to Human Rights Watch, the Eritrean government's human rights record is among the worst in the world.<br>
As of 2021 Reporters Without Borders considers the country to have the overall worst press freedom in the world,<br>
 as all media publications and access are heavily controlled by the government.<br>

Haiti , officially the Republic of Haiti, is a country located on the island of Hispaniola in the Greater Antilles archipelago of the Caribbean Sea, <br>
to the east of Cuba and Jamaica and south of The Bahamas and the Turks and Caicos Islands. <br>
It occupies the western three-eighths of the island which it shares with the Dominican Republic.<br>
To its south-west lies the small island of Navassa Island, which is claimed by Haiti but is disputed as a United States territory under federal administration.<br>
 Haiti is 27,750 square kilometers (10,714 sq mi) in size, the third largest country in the Caribbean by area, <br>
 and has an estimated population of 11.4 million, making it the most populous country in the Caribbean.<br>

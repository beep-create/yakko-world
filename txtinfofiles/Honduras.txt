Honduras, officially the Republic of Honduras, is a country in Central America. <br>
The republic of Honduras is bordered to the west by Guatemala, to the southwest by El Salvador, to the southeast by Nicaragua, <br>
to the south by the Pacific Ocean at the Gulf of Fonseca, and to the north by the Gulf of Honduras, a large inlet of the Caribbean Sea.<br>
 Its capital and largest city is Tegucigalpa.<br>
 Honduras spans about 112,492 km2 (43,433 sq mi) and has a population exceeding 9 million.<br>
 Honduras became independent in 1821 and has since been a republic, although it has consistently endured much social strife and political instability,<br>
 and remains one of the poorest countries in the Western Hemisphere. <br>

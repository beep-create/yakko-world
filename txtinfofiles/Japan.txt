Japan is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean. <br>
It is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, and extends from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea and Taiwan in the south.<br>
 Part of the Ring of Fire, Japan spans an archipelago of 6852 islands covering 377,975 square kilometers (145,937 sq mi); <br>
 the five main islands are Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa. <br>
 Tokyo is Japan's capital and largest city; other major cities include Yokohama, Osaka, Nagoya, Sapporo, Fukuoka, Kobe, and Kyoto.<br>
 About three-fourths of the country's terrain is mountainous, concentrating its population of 125.47 million on narrow coastal plains. <br>

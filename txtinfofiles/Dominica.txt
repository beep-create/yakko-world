Dominica, officially the Commonwealth of Dominica, is an island country in the Caribbean. <br>
 The capital, Roseau, is located on the western side of the island.  <br>
 It is geographically situated as part of the Windward Islands chain in the Lesser Antilles archipelago in the Caribbean Sea.  <br>
 The island is nestled by the Republic of France with Guadeloupe to the northwest and Martinique to the south-southeast.  <br>
 Its area is 750 km2 (290 sq mi), and the highest point is Morne Diablotins, at 1ique; Dominican Creole French: Dominik),447 m (4,747 ft) in elevation.  <br>
 The population was 71,293 at the 2011 census. <br>

Monaco, officially the Principality of Monaco, is a sovereign city-state and microstate on the French Riviera close to the Italian region of Liguria, in Western Europe.<br>
 It is bordered by France to the north, east and west, and the Mediterranean Sea to the south. <br>
 The principality is home to 38,682 residents, of whom 9,486 are Monégasque nationals;<br>
  it is widely recognised for being one of the most expensive and wealthiest places in the world. <br>
  The official language is French, although Monégasque (a dialect of Ligurian), Italian and English are spoken and understood by a sizeable group.<br>
  Despite Monaco's independence and separate foreign policy, its defence is the responsibility of France. <br>
  However, Monaco does maintain two small military units.<br>

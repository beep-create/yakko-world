Cyprus, officially called the Republic of Cyprus, is an island nation in the eastern Mediterranean Sea. <br>
It is the third largest and third most populous island in the Mediterranean, and is located south of Turkey; <br>
west of Syria; northwest of Lebanon, Israel and the Gaza Strip; north of Egypt; and southeast of Greece. <br>
Nicosia is the country's capital and largest city.<br>
The currency of Cyprus is the Euro. <br>
As of 2018, the population of Cyprus is 1,189,265. <br>

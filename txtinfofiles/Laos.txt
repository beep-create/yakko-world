Laos, officially the Lao People's Democratic Republic, is a socialist state and the only landlocked country in Southeast Asia. <br>
At the heart of the Indochinese Peninsula, Laos is bordered by Myanmar and China to the northwest, Vietnam to the east, <br>
Cambodia to the southeast and Thailand to the west and southwest.<br>
 Its capital and largest city is Vientiane.<br>
 Laos has a total area of 237,955 km2 (91,875 sq mi).<br>
 The currency is the Lao Kip.<br>
 Laos has been referred to as one of Southeast Asia and Pacific's fastest growing economies by the World Bank with annual GDP growth averaging 7.4% since 2009.<br>

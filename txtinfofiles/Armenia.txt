 Armenia, officially the Republic of Armenia, is a landlocked country located in the Armenian Highlands of Western Asia. <br>
 It is a part of the Caucasus region; and is bordered by Turkey to the west, Georgia to the north, the Lachin corridor under a Russian peacekeeping force, <br>
 and Azerbaijan to the east, and Iran and the Azerbaijani exclave of Nakhchivan to the south.  <br>
 Yerevan is the capital and largest city. <br>
 The currency is the Armenian dram. <br>
 The population of Armenia is 2,963,900, as of 2020. <br>

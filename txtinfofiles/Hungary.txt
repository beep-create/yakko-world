Hungary is a landlocked country in Central Europe.<br>
 Spanning 93,030 square kilometres (35,920 sq mi) of the Carpathian Basin, it is bordered by Slovakia to the north, Ukraine to the northeast, <br>
 Romania to the east and southeast, Serbia to the south, Croatia and Slovenia to the southwest, and Austria to the west. <br>
 Hungary has a population of 10 million, mostly ethnic Hungarians and a significant Romani minority. <br>
 Hungarian, the official language, is the world's most widely spoken Uralic language, and among the few non-Indo-European languages widely spoken in Europe.<br>
  Budapest is the country's capital and largest city; other major urban areas include Debrecen, Szeged, Miskolc, Pécs, and Győr.<br>

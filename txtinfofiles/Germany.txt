Germany, officially the Federal Republic of Germany, is a country in Central Europe. <br>
It is the second-most populous country in Europe after Russia, and the most populous member state of the European Union. <br>
Germany is situated between the Baltic and North seas to the north, and the Alps to the south;<br>
 covering an area of 357,022 square kilometres (137,847 sq mi), with a population of over 83 million within its 16 constituent states. <br>
 It borders Denmark to the north, Poland and the Czech Republic to the east, Austria and Switzerland to the south, <br>
 and France, Luxembourg, Belgium, and the Netherlands to the west. <br>
 The nation's capital and largest city is Berlin, and its financial centre is Frankfurt; the largest urban area is the Ruhr.<br>
 Germanys curreny is the Euro. <br>

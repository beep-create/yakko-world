Australia, officially the Commonwealth of Australia, is a sovereign country comprising the mainland of the Australian continent, <br>
 the island of Tasmania, and numerous smaller islands. <br>
 It is the largest country in Oceania and the world's sixth-largest country. <br>
 Australia's population of nearly 26 million, in an area of 7,617,930 square kilometres (2,941,300 sq mi), <br>
 is highly urbanised and heavily concentrated on the eastern seaboard. <br>
 Canberra is the nation's capital, while the largest city is Sydney, and other major metropolitan areas are Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and Adelaide. <br>
 The currency of Australia is the Australian dollar. <br>

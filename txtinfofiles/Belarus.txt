Belarus,officially the Republic of Belarus, is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe. <br>
t is bordered by Russia to the east and northeast, Ukraine to the south, Poland to the west, and Lithuania and Latvia to the northwest. <br>
Covering an area of 207,600 square kilometres (80,200 sq mi) and with a population of 9.3 million, <br>
Belarus is the thirteenth-largest and the twentieth-most populous country in Europe. <br>
The country is administratively divided into seven regions. Minsk is the capital and largest city. <br>
Belarus's currency is the Belarussian Ruble. <br>

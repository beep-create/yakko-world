Argentina, officially the Argentine Republic, is a country in the southern half of South America. <br>
It shares the bulk of the Southern Cone with Chile to the west, and is also bordered by Bolivia and Paraguay to the north, <br>
Brazil to the northeast, Uruguay and the South Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Drake Passage to the south. <br>
Argentina covers an area of 2,780,400 km2 (1,073,500 sq mi), and is the largest Spanish-speaking nation in the world. <br>
It is the second-largest country in South America after Brazil, the fourth-largest country in the Americas, and the eighth-largest country in the world. <br>
Argentina is subdivided into twenty-three provinces, and one autonomous city, which is the federal capital and largest city of the nation, Buenos Aires. <br>
The provinces and the capital have their own constitutions, but exist under a federal system. <br>
Argentina claims sovereignty over a part of Antarctica, the Falkland Islands and South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands. <br>
The currency of Argentina is the Argentine peso. <br>

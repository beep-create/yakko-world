Latvia, officially known as the Republic of Latvia, is a country in the Baltic region of Northern Europe. <br>
It is one of the Baltic states; and is bordered by Estonia to the north, Lithuania to the south, Russia to the east, <br>
Belarus to the southeast, and shares a maritime border with Sweden to the west. <br>
Latvia covers an area of 64,589 km2 (24,938 sq mi), with a population of 1.9 million. <br>
The country has a temperate seasonal climate.<br>
 Its capital and largest city is Riga. <br>
Latvians belong to the ethno-linguistic group of the Balts; and speak Latvian, one of the only two surviving Baltic languages. <br>
Russians are a prominent minority in the country, at some 24% of the population.<br>
